# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Welcome diverse perspectives
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Be patient and inclusive

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive comments about identity, appearance, or experience
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling or personal attacks
- Publishing private information

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Comment removal
- Temporary ban
- Permanent removal from community

## Reporting

Report concerns to: conduct@example.com
